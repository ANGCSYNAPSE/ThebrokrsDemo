06

KYC flow and step points

Ask only for the verification needed by the selected service. Give 10 points after each successful step.

User continues serviceThe chosen action needs verification
check profile→
Which check is missing?
complete one→
Verify stepSubmit and wait for success
success→
+10 pointsOne-time reward for this KYC step
return→
Resume serviceContinue exactly where user stopped
Phone verification+10A separate one-time KYC reward. A new user can also receive the 100-point welcome reward.
PAN verification+10Reward once after the verification provider confirms success.
Identity / Aadhaar check+10Store a token/status, not unnecessary identity data.
Bank verification+10Reward once after account ownership is confirmed.
Address verification+10Only ask when the selected service needs it.
Video KYC+10Reward once after review is approved, not after upload.
Anti-abuse rule: The 100 welcome points and the 10 phone-KYC points are separate ledger entries. Each KYC capability can earn its 10-point reward only once per user. Re-upload, refresh, rejection, or repeated verification does not earn again.

Points PendingFor inviter and/or friend
VALID: After the refund/fraud window, pending points become available and both users are notified.
NOT VALID: Show the exact reason—existing account, expired link, self-referral, duplicate identity/device/payment, or action not completed.
LINK CREATED→CLICKED→ACCOUNT CREATED→WAITING FOR ACTION→QUALIFIED→POINTS AVAILABLE
Referral rule: Do not reward only for sharing or installing. Reward when the referred person completes the exact action published in that campaign. Start with one referral level—no chain or MLM rewards.
